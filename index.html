How to Play

🎮 What is this game?

Trivia Guess is a quick, addictive trivia game where you answer questions on all kinds of topics — geography, movies, science, you name it. Guess letters, pick the right answer, and build up as big a score as you can before you run out of hearts. Simple to pick up, surprisingly hard to put down!

❓ How a round works

Every question comes in one of three flavors:

🟢 Easy — It's just like Hangman. You'll see a blank space for each letter of the answer, and you tap letters on the on-screen keyboard to guess. Get it right letter by letter until the whole word appears.

🟡 Medium — Same hangman game as Easy, but you get a free first letter to start you off. A little help to point you in the right direction.

🔴 Hard — No letter-guessing here — just four answers to choose from. Tap the one you think is right.

❤️ Hearts

You start every game with 3 hearts. Get a question wrong — run out of letter guesses on a hangman question, or pick the wrong multiple-choice answer — and you lose one. Run out of all three hearts and the game ends. But don't worry, you can jump right back in and try again whenever you like.

💡 Stuck? Use "Show options"

Stuck on a hangman question (Easy or Medium)? Tap "Show options" and it'll turn into multiple choice for you — four answers, pick the right one. It costs you a couple of letter guesses, but it can save your heart when you're really not sure. You get one "Show options" per question, so use it wisely.

🌍 Categories & Mix

Pick a category you love — Geography, Movies, Science, Animals, and more — to focus your questions on that topic. Or tap Mix to get a random blend of everything. Either way, you'll never know exactly what's coming next.

🎯 Difficulty modes

When you pick a category, you'll also choose how you want to play:

📈 Progressive (recommended if you're just starting out) — Starts nice and easy, then gets tougher the better you're doing. It feels like leveling up as you go.

🎲 Random — No telling what's coming. Could be easy, could be hard — every question is a surprise.

🟢 Easy — Every question is easy. Perfect for a relaxed, chill session.

🟡 Medium — Every question is medium. A steady, fair challenge.

🔴 Hard — Every question is hard, multiple choice only. For when you want to really test yourself.

🔥 Streaks

Every correct answer in a row builds your streak — and the bigger your streak, the more bonus points each answer is worth. Get one wrong, though, and your streak resets to zero. So a long streak feels amazing... but it's worth protecting!

Here's roughly what you earn:

  • Easy question: 10 points
  • Medium question: 20 points
  • Hard question: 30 points
  • Plus a streak bonus: 5 points × your current streak

So the longer your streak, the juicier your points get — a 5-answer streak on a medium question could earn you 20 + 25 = 45 points in one go!

🌟 Daily Challenge

Once a day, there's a special Daily Challenge — one hard question, worth double points, and only one try. Answer it right and your daily streak grows, which makes tomorrow's challenge worth even more. But miss a day, and that streak resets — so try to check in every day and keep it alive!

🏆 Chasing your best

Your best score and best streak are always saved, right there on the home screen. Every run is a fresh chance to beat your own record — good luck!

⚙️ Settings

Want to turn the volume down, mute the sound, or turn off vibration? Tap the gear icon ⚙️ on the home screen any time to adjust things to your liking.
